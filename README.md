# Economics and Public Policy Analytics

This repository contains applied data analysis case studies focused on
**macroeconomic indicators and public policy outcomes** using real-world datasets.

The goal of these projects is not forecasting alone but **interpretation**, understanding how economic signals reflect policy decisions, structural changes,and real-world constraints.
---

## Case Studies

- **Inflation: Cooling or Coping?**  
  Analyzes inflation trends to assess whether observed slowdowns indicategenuine cooling or temporary stabilization.
  
- **State-wise Corporate Tax Analysis**  
  A comparative analysis of corporate tax patterns across states.

---

## Why This Repository Exists

Economic data is often used for high-level narratives without sufficient context.
These case studies aim to bridge that gap by grounding analysis in:
- structural trends
- policy interpretation
- regional comparisons
